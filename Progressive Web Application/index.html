Natural Disaster Preparedness PWA

Web Application Description

The web application will provide the user with information on the natural disasters, the different categories of disasters, and the ways on preparing for and adapting to these disasters. Information on health and public safety service center locations are also provided by the application to users.

Features:

Disaster Preparedness Information

Different categories of disasters are described in detail such as the potential hazards they bring and the harm and devastation a calamity can inflict. The resources needed and the methods for preparing and adjusting to the conditions during these events are also described in the web application.

Health and Public Safety Services

Users are able to find the nearest health, law enforcement, or other public safety service center. Information about these locations include the location name, service type, contact numbers, address, a small map with details of the nearest landmarks, and the longitude and latitude of the location. This data will be stored client-side using IndexedDB. Users may also set their location in order to make it easier to find the nearest service centers.

Push Notifications

A push notification will welcome the user once subscribed and send messages encouraging the user to read content provided by the web application. Users will also be notified of the nearest health and public safety services once they have set their current location.
